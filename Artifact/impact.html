The Impact of World Wide Web

Web as a Source of Information

"Knowledge is power". The web empowers individuals as it can easily reach a worldwide audience. Anyone can now publish material that would never been published in the past, without the help of Web, and it is instantly available to millions of people. The web now is like an online library and thus it is a great source of information. Newspapers, journals and books can be accessed online. With the help of World Wide Web, people can now do their research on the web without actually visiting a library. The only thing that we need is to learn how to utilize search engine effectively.

Web as a Way to Communicate

With the help of the Web, you can create new social contacts all over the world. With this environment, it can helps people, due to specific reasons, to find friends in the real life. Sometimes it is also easier for people, especially the nerdy one, who are afraid to interact with people directly to communicate with new friends. Hence, the Web had removed time and place boundaries.

Nowadays, social networking sites are the preferred platforms for these kind of activities. According to Silicon Alley Insider, the number of social network user has surpassed the number of e-mail users in July 2019.

Web as Source of Entertainment

The web offers several sources of multimedia. Multimedia comprise of text, images, animated graphics (animation), audio and video. People can now easily access all sort of multimedia from the Web e.g: Youtube.

Furthermore, with the Web people can now play games online. Some games are downloadable whereas others can be played even in real time with other players. There are even some games that involve many players online simultaneously (MMORPGs).

Web as Venue for Commerce

Several businesses now have a virtual store on the Web that enable customers to browse and even purchase online. Auction houses, like eBay have emerged and it allows people directly sell their merchandise without maintaining physical store. Shopping agents have appeared like Tripadvisor and Expedia, assist shoppers to find the best deals available. In order to facilitate payment over the Web several payment gateway services have also emerged. Gateway such as PayPal and BitCoin is rarely unheard of.
